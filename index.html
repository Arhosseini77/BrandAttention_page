Practical Deep Learning Approach for Logo Detection, Saliency-Map Prediction, and Brand Placement Optimization

Alireza Hosseini, Kiana Hooshanfar, Pouria Omrani, Reza Toosi, Ramin Toosi

Paper Code Dataset

Abstract

In a competitive business landscape, brand visibility, particularly through logos, plays a critical role in consumer perception and product success. We introduces an advanced framework leveraging YOLOv8 for logo detection in advertising and packaging images. A novel saliency prediction model, tailored for these contexts and incorporating text maps, significantly outperforms existing models in predicting visual attention. The framework quantitatively evaluates the attention drawn to brand logos, integrating logo detection with saliency map generation to yield a comprehensive brand attention score. Supported by a unique dataset examining 12 cognitive hypotheses, our approach not only validates previous theories on logo placement but also introduces new propositions for optimization in logo packaging placement and design. This study offers significant tools and insights for enhancing brand visibility, with practical implications.

1. Logo-Detection

This module focuses on detecting brand logos in images using the YOLOv8 model. It utilizes two datasets for training: FoodLogoDet-1500 and LogoDet-3K.
